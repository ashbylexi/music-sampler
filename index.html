Glass Animals

Who Are They?

Glass Animals is an English psychadelic rock band that formed in 2020 in Oxford, England. The main singer is Dave Bayley, who also doubles as the songwriter and producer. On drums is Joe Seaward, Ed Irwin-Singer doubles as bass and keys and Drew MacFarlane is the guitarist. Over the past 10 years, Glass Animals has sold countless records, the first of which was Zaba which was released in 2014. Zaba featured the single Gooey which went platnum in the United States.
